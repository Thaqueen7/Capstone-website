Indigneous Futures

I would like to gather our elders and our youth in the same area working together to bring our community closer. Having our youth and elders work together but could not only improve their education it would encourage communication. Here are some of the ideas I have.

  • Have the youth working with the elders
  • Learning trades like cooking while they cook for the elders
  • Start an elders mentoring program
  • Recreation and story time
